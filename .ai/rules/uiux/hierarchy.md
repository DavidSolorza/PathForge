# UIUX Hierarchy Rules

1. Page title: text-2xl font-semibold
2. Section title: text-lg font-semibold
3. Card header: text-base font-semibold
4. Body: text-sm text-neutral-700
5. Caption: text-xs text-neutral-500
6. Primary actions: button with bg-primary-600
7. Secondary actions: button with variant="outline" or variant="ghost"
